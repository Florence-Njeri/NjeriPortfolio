Hi, I'm Florence!

My name is Florence Njeri. I am a Software Engineer by profession and for the past three years I have developed for the web, mobile, SDET and technical writing.

I currently work as a Software Development Engineer in Test where I analyze bugs and create detailed reports, perform manual and automated regression testing for features to ensure existing features don't break. What I love the most about my job is that I get to work with the whole application, therefore I know and understand how each feature and functionality works which helps me better support and onboard the developers when they are working on new features. This deep knowledge of the application also helps me succeed in my role as an SDET.

Writing comes naturally to me. I begun writing early on in my career documenting fixes to bugs and challenging problems I had solved. This has latter evolved to me documenting projects and writing articles that teach other developers various features and functionalities they can leverage. My job as an SDET and educational background in software engineering has highly contributed to me being able to write deeply and well researched technical articles and dicumentation.

Fun fact:I am a book collector; so far I have close to 60 books and counting and I hoard lots of stationery :)
